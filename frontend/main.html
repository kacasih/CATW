About the game

Get ready for an adventure like no other with our purrfect feline hero!
With its fluffy fur and determined spirit, this cute kitty is on a mission
to jump over obstacles and collect rewards like a true boss. But watch
out - the journey won't be easy. Our hero is up against two foes:
a grumpy dog and a poisonous mushroom on the ground.
To avoid getting into sticky situations, you'll need to show off your
awesome skills! Imagine you're the captain of this kitty's adventure
ship, guiding it through the obstacle-filled sea.
As you guide your furry friend on this wild ride, you'll notice
sparkling fish scattered all around. These aren't just any fish -
they're like shiny treasures waiting for you to grab! Catch those
fish and your cat will be over the moon. And guess what?
This adventure is happening right in front of you, on your computer
or tablet. No need for a magic portal or a secret door.
Just tap, click, or press and you're in the world of "Catventure"!
